Interpretation:

The bar chart illustrates the distribution of male and female penguins across the three islands Biscoe, Dream and Torgersen. There are three species: Adelie, Chinstrap and Gentoo. The Adelie penguin appears on all three islands, indicating that it is a more ecologically flexible species, capable of inhabiting diverse environments. In contrast, Chinstrap penguins are only found on Dream Island and Gentoo penguins are only found on Biscoe Island , suggesting a more restricted habitat preference. Gentoo and Chinstrap penguins are completely absent from Torgersen Island, implying that this location may not provide suitable conditions for that species

The number of weeks in charts and peak rank alone cannot fully reflect a song‚Äôs popularity: compare a song which was a one hit (aka one week) wonder with possibly peak rank 1 and a song ranking 10th place but for multiple weeks. Create a new combined popularity_score metric for songs which is calculated by dividing the number of weeks the song has been in the charts by its peak rank.
spotify_data$popularity_score <- spotify_data$weeks_on_chart / spotify_data$peak_rank
